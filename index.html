How Does This Work?

Upon conditionally approving your commercial real estate loan, HomeStreet sets the borrower's principal contact person along with a loan number. This person receives an email with temporary credentials to verify themselves. Upon successful verification, they can upload necessary loan artifacts needed to underwrite the loan. This principal contact can delegate others to upload documents.
